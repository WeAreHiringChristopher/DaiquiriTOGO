About Daiquiri To-Go

Daiquiri TO-GO is a family-owned location that opened recently in the relaxing town of Shepherd, Texas. While we specialize in providing customers the option to purchase daiquiris to go in sealed containers. Customers also have the option of purchasing and enjoying daiquiris, beer, or alcohol-free beverages on site. We welcome families as well by offering kids size portions of our specials and kid friendly drinks. We are ever expanding by offering new drinks, specials, and locations to our community. Please come in to enjoy our drinks, food specials, and southern charm. We look forward to meeting you and your family.
